Yakisoba

Yakisoba is a stir-fried noodle dish that typically includes meat or seafood, as well as vegetables such as cabbage, carrots, onions, and bean sprouts.

These ingredients are all cooked together in a thick, sweet-savory sauce, and the final dish is usually topped with aonori (dried green seaweed) and red pickled ginger for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • Yakisoba noodles
  • Vegetables
  • Protein(s)
  • Oil
  • Yakisoba Sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Cut the ingredients into bite-size pieces.
  2. Warm up the noodles and transfer them to a dish.
  3. Cook the protein, followed by the tough vegetables, and then the soft vegetables.
  4. Put back the noodles and season them with Yakisoba Sauce.
  5. Toss them all together and serve.
